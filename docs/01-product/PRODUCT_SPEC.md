# Campio Product Spec

## One-line Definition

Campio is a platform where university students discover every opportunity in one place.

## Core Problem

University students miss valuable opportunities because information is scattered across:

- school websites
- department notices
- Instagram posts
- KakaoTalk group chats
- Everytime
- LinkedIn
- company career pages
- government websites
- professor emails
- senior networks

The result:
students do not know what exists, what is relevant, or what deadline is approaching.

## Core Solution

Campio collects, organizes, filters, and recommends opportunities for students.

## Opportunity Categories

- Internship
- Full-time job
- Contest
- Scholarship
- Exchange program
- External activity
- Startup support
- Government support
- Seminar
- Campus event
- Mentoring
- Senior review

## Product Positioning

Campio is not a job board.
Campio is not a mentoring-only service.
Campio is not a school notice board.

Campio is:

> A premium opportunity discovery platform for university students.

## MVP Value Proposition

A student can open Campio and immediately see:

- what is new
- what is closing soon
- what is popular on campus
- what matches their school/major/interests
- what they saved
- what they should apply to next

## Primary Users

### Student

Wants to discover opportunities and not miss deadlines.

### Senior / Mentor

Can answer questions, share experiences, and help students use opportunities better.

### Admin

Creates and manages opportunities.

## Success Criteria

MVP is successful if a student can:

1. Sign up
2. Set school, major, and interests
3. Browse personalized opportunity feed
4. Filter opportunities
5. View details
6. Save opportunities
7. Record application status
8. Ask/read community questions
