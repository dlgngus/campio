# Home Screen

## Purpose

Home is the most important screen.
It must immediately show the student's next opportunities.

## Header

```text
Good afternoon, Ryan.
Find your next opportunity.
```

## Top Summary Cards

- Closing soon
- New this week
- Recommended for your major
- Saved opportunities

## Sections

### 1. Recommended For You

Personalized opportunities based on:
- school
- major
- grade
- interests
- saved behavior

### 2. Closing Soon

Deadline-first section.
Sort by nearest deadline.

### 3. Popular On Your Campus

Opportunities saved or viewed by students from same school.

### 4. New This Week

Recently added opportunities.

### 5. Scholarship Picks

Dedicated scholarship section.

### 6. Internship Picks

Dedicated internship section.

## Required Components

- FeaturedOpportunityCard
- OpportunityCard
- SectionHeader
- CategoryChip
- DeadlineBadge

## Empty State

If recommendations are empty:

```text
No recommendations yet.
Pick more interests to improve your feed.
```
